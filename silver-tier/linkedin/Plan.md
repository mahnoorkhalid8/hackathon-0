## 3. Content Pillars

### Pillar 1: Educational Content (40%)
**Purpose:** Establish thought leadership and provide value

**Content Types:**
- How-to guides and tutorials
- Industry insights and trends
- Best practices and frameworks
- Common mistakes to avoid

**Example Topics:**
- "5 Ways AI automation consulting Can Transform Your Business"
- "The Ultimate Guide to [Industry Topic] for small businesses"
- "3 Common Mistakes small businesses Make (And How to Fix Them)"

### Pillar 2: Social Proof & Case Studies (30%)
**Purpose:** Build credibility and demonstrate results

**Content Types:**
- Client success stories
- Before/after transformations
- ROI calculations and metrics
- Testimonials and reviews

**Example Topics:**
- "How We Helped [Client] Achieve [Result] in [Timeframe]"
- "Case Study: [Specific Problem] to [Specific Solution]"
- "Real Results: [Metric] Improvement for small businesses"

### Pillar 3: Engagement & Community (20%)
**Purpose:** Build relationships and foster discussion

**Content Types:**
- Questions and polls
- Industry discussions
- Controversial opinions (professional)
- Behind-the-scenes content

**Example Topics:**
- "What's your biggest challenge with [Topic]?"
- "Hot take: [Controversial but professional opinion]"
- "Let's discuss: The future of [Industry Topic]"

### Pillar 4: Promotional Content (10%)
**Purpose:** Drive conversions and generate leads

**Content Types:**
- Service offerings
- Free resources and lead magnets
- Webinar announcements
- Limited-time offers

**Example Topics:**
- "Free Download: [Resource Name] for small businesses"
- "Join Our Free Webinar: [Topic]"
- "Limited Spots: [Service/Offer]"

## 5. Call-to-Action Strategy

### Soft CTAs (80% of posts)
- "Save this for later"
- "Share with someone who needs this"
- "Comment your thoughts below"
- "Follow for more [topic] content"
- "Tag someone who should see this"

### Medium CTAs (15% of posts)
- "DM me for more information"
- "Download our free guide (link in comments)"
- "Join our newsletter for weekly tips"
- "Book a free consultation (link in profile)"

### Hard CTAs (5% of posts)
- "Limited spots available - DM to claim yours"
- "Special offer for the next 10 people"
- "Ready to get started? Let's talk"

### CTA Placement
- **In Post:** Natural, conversational CTAs
- **First Comment:** Links and detailed CTAs
- **Profile:** Clear value proposition and booking link

## 6. Lead Conversion Flow

### Stage 1: Awareness
**Goal:** Get on their radar

**Actions:**
- Consistent, valuable content posting
- Engage with target audience's posts
- Join relevant LinkedIn groups
- Use strategic hashtags

**Metrics:** Profile views, post impressions, follower growth

### Stage 2: Interest
**Goal:** Build trust and credibility

**Actions:**
- Share case studies and results
- Provide free resources (guides, templates)
- Respond to all comments and messages
- Share behind-the-scenes content

**Metrics:** Engagement rate, saves, shares, DMs

### Stage 3: Consideration
**Goal:** Position as the solution

**Actions:**
- Offer free consultation or audit
- Share detailed case studies
- Address common objections
- Provide social proof

**Metrics:** Consultation bookings, resource downloads

### Stage 4: Conversion
**Goal:** Close the deal

**Actions:**
- Personalized outreach to engaged prospects
- Limited-time offers or bonuses
- Clear next steps and onboarding
- Follow-up sequence

**Metrics:** Leads generated, conversion rate, revenue

### Lead Nurturing Sequence
1. **Day 1:** Connection request with personalized note
2. **Day 3:** Share valuable resource (no ask)
3. **Day 7:** Engage with their content
4. **Day 10:** Share relevant case study
5. **Day 14:** Soft pitch - free consultation offer
6. **Day 21:** Follow-up if no response
7. **Day 30:** Final touchpoint or move to long-term nurture

## 7. Metrics to Track

### Content Performance
- **Impressions:** Total reach of your content
- **Engagement Rate:** (Likes + Comments + Shares) / Impressions
- **Click-Through Rate:** Clicks on links / Impressions
- **Save Rate:** Saves / Impressions
- **Share Rate:** Shares / Impressions

**Target:** 5-10% engagement rate, 1-2% CTR

### Audience Growth
- **Follower Growth:** New followers per week/month
- **Connection Growth:** New connections per week/month
- **Profile Views:** Weekly/monthly profile views
- **Search Appearances:** How often you appear in searches

**Target:** 100+ new connections/month, 500+ profile views/month

### Lead Generation
- **Inbound DMs:** Messages from prospects
- **Consultation Bookings:** Free calls scheduled
- **Resource Downloads:** Lead magnet downloads
- **Email Signups:** Newsletter subscribers

**Target:** 10 qualified leads/month

### Conversion Metrics
- **Lead-to-Customer Rate:** % of leads that become customers
- **Average Deal Size:** Revenue per customer
- **Customer Acquisition Cost:** Cost to acquire one customer
- **Lifetime Value:** Total revenue per customer

**Target:** 20-30% lead-to-customer rate

### Engagement Metrics
- **Comments per Post:** Average comments received
- **Response Rate:** % of comments you respond to
- **Response Time:** Average time to respond
- **DM Conversion Rate:** % of DMs that become leads

**Target:** 100% response rate, <24 hour response time

## 10. Best Practices

### Content Creation
- **Hook:** First line must grab attention
- **Value:** Every post should provide actionable value
- **Formatting:** Use line breaks, emojis (sparingly), and bullet points
- **Length:** 1,300-2,000 characters for maximum reach
- **Visuals:** Include images, carousels, or videos when possible

### Engagement
- **Respond Fast:** Reply to comments within first hour
- **Be Genuine:** Authentic engagement beats generic comments
- **Add Value:** Don't just say "Great post!" - add insights
- **Tag Strategically:** Tag relevant people/companies when appropriate
- **Timing:** Post when your audience is most active

### Profile Optimization
- **Headline:** Clear value proposition (not just job title)
- **About Section:** Story + benefits + CTA
- **Featured Section:** Showcase best content and resources
- **Experience:** Results-focused, not just responsibilities
- **Recommendations:** Request from satisfied clients
